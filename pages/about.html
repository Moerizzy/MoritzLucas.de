About me

Information About me

After graduating from high school I began to study geography, because maps and spatial relationships fascinated me from an early age. During my studies I had my first contact with GIS and remote sensing. This inspired me to specialise in this field and to pursue with a Master's degree in Geoinformatics.

In the time between my geography studies I worked as a student assistant in a city planning office and did an internship in this field. Within my geoinformatics studies I gained first work experience at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence. Currently I am working as tutor and student assistant at the University of Osnabrück.
